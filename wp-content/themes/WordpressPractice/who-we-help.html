Who We Help

Revenue Cycle Services for Hospitalist Groups:

RCMMatter will handle the billing for 2-50 doctors or more for the Hospitalist Groups. Our expert billing staff are well versed in in-patient coding and billing. RCMMatter ensure continuous improvements in quality assurance and process management that minimizes mistakes and maximizes equal reimbursement to the providers.

Urgent Cares & Ambulatory Surgical Centers:

We have several years of experience serving in UCC & ASC. RCMMatter is the ideal fit company with UCC & ASC expertise, as well as the necessary resources to work directly with front-office staff. Ensuring maximum efficiency from the beginning to the end of the revenue cycle.

Private Practices:

The foundation for a successful private practice lies in extracting steady, reliable income from a growing, dispersed payer mix. This steadiness includes a tightly organized method of medical billing, in which every part of the revenue process is provided special consideration by appropriately qualified and supervised staff. We at the RCMMatter take pride in our robust full-service medical billing operations for the private practices.

Help in Outsourcing:

Medical billing is one of the most time consuming and frustrating tasks that often diverts the attention of medical practitioners from their core expertise. Why? Because they often end up overseeing the billing themselves, or at the very least, being heavily involved in the process. RCMMatter help you to outsource your entire medical billing to experts.
